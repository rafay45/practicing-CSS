I am madly in love with you

YOU👰🏻‍♀️

You are everything to me. You are an incredibly beautiful girl 🥰. You are lovely in every way your face is stunning MashaAllah and your character is just as beautiful My Life 🙈🌍. You will also be an amazing wife to me. You are everything to me, My Love🥺. I love you so much and I want to marry you and make you mine forever. And I know you want the same 😌💫. You are perfect in every way you love me, you also get mad at me, but I know that’s just another way of showing your love 😘💞. Thank you so much for loving me this much. You are my Cute shi guriya and the reason behind all my happiness 🤗🎉. You are also my Choti shi Munni . You are my entire world ❣️🫂.

Your Eyes🙈

MashaAllah MashaAllah🫀 what can I even say Your eyes are so beautiful so mesmerizing 🥺. My heart just wants to keep looking at them forever. I want to drown in them I want to lose myself in them 😭🫀. I wish I could just sit you in front of me and keep staring into your eyes 🙈. I want to see my reflection in them 🤧. I have so many dreams, so many desires with you. InshaAllah they will all come true because I have faith in my ALLAH and in my prayers 🤲🏻🥀.

Your Hair✨

MashaAllah, the way I call them Zulfen because they are my Zulfen 🤗🥰. I’m in love with them too I just want to hold them in my hands and lose myself in them 😫. I want to feel their mesmerizing fragrance and InshaAllah that moment is coming very soon, Jaan 🥺. I keep praying so much that Allah brings you to me quickly so that I can fulfill all the dreams I have woven with you. These silky, dreamy Zulfen they are my life MashaAllah. You’ll let me play with them right, Jaan? 🤧🥺🥺
